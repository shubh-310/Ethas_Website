Annual Maintenance Contract

An Annual Maintenance Contract by Etha's, is just what you need. Sign up with us, we play the role as your extended IT Team to keep your IT infrastructure in perfect condition. Etha's offer Comprehensive and Non-Comprehensive Annual Maintance Contract as mentioned below;

Non - Comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contract

In a Non- Comprehensive AMC, the cost of spare parts replacement must be borne by the customer. This usually means only service. Preventive maintenance comes under this and a consultant will advise on hardware bread down.

Comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contract

In a comprehensive AMC, the analysis of breakdowns/faults in the hardware and repairing/service is taken care of by the service provider. This includes spare parts replacement. Apart from this, preventive maintenance is also done, on a pre-determined/agreed-to frequency. Note: It excludes all physical mishandling or damages.

Our IT Infrastructure - Annual Maintenance Contract Services

Our IT AMC Services includes Comprehensive and Non-Comprehensive AMC's for Hardware, Desktops, Laptops, Servers, Printers, Scanners, Networking devices,etc. We also provide high level of Performance Upgradation in your IT Infrastructure.

Hardware Upgradation

Hardware Upgradation involves adding new hardware or updating software to increase the functionality of a system. However, system upgrades also have many less-known benefits such as an increase in the lifespan, better efficiency of computer equipment, Improved system security, Extended warranty,etc.

Hardware Maintenance And Troubleshooting

It includes repairing and replacing faulty RAM, Hard Disk, Graphic Card and its Components. Cleaning dusts from RAM and Video carts slot/ports and from cooling fan and Tightening cable and jumpers on motherboard and/or components. Entire Hardware of your Systems are maintained here.

Server Maintenance And Troubleshooting

It is "a routine for periodically inspecting" with the goal of "noticing small problems and fixing them before major ones develop." It includes Verify Backups, Monitor RAID Alarms, Monitor Remote Management Tools, Inspect Equipments, Monitor Server Room Humidity and Temperature,etc.

Software Installation

A well-designed software installation tool can pragmatically address the growing number of requests through an automated software installation tool. It mainly includes the installation of System Softwares and Application Softwares as per client requirements.

Performance Optimization

It is also known as “Performance Tuning”, is usually an iterative approach to making and then monitoring modifications to a device. The actual Performance Optimization requirements will depend on the level of complexity of a project and the experience of the development team.

Periodic Checkup of IT Infrastructure

Periodic infrastructure audit helps keep it in check - Resilient, Performant, Reliable, Optimal,etc. The businesses rarely get their IT infrastructure sorted out from the start.
